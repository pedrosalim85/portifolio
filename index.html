Hello, my name is Pedro

I´m a sound and image producer, living in Juiz de Fora, Brazil

About me

I was born in may 1985, and graduated in Cinema, Television and Digital Medias in 2005. My master degree is in movie direction, concluded in Paris, 2008. Another master degree is in Social Ecology, concluded in Rio de Janeiro, 2015. Nowadays I am studying Sound Engineering and Computer programing.

  • Pedro Salim Miranda

With 20 years of experience as video editor, I specialized in video-projection for spectacles, mainly for theater. Also I worked for television in several shows: about literature, turism, cooking and the news. I am also a musician and a sound technician, beeing able to record music tracks. Since 2020 I own my label JFK7, in which I produce independent artists from the local scene.
